WHO WE ARE Deep Worlds was founded in 2022 by a small team of passionate game developers base in Geneva, Switzerland. From Esport players, modders to enthusiast community members, our team comes from all around the world. We aim to challenge genre norms by pushing immersion on a new level. Our debut title, Black Light, will help us build the grounds for the ambitions we have to change the gaming industry.
OUR VALUES

PASSION

Above all, we are gaming creatives, developers and enthusiasts who thrive to inspire people with our games.

CHALLENGE

Pushing gaming on a new level is not an easy task. But we surely use our curiosity, creativity and our heart to try and achieve this goal.

TRANSPARENCY

Transparency with our players and transparency with our employees. Communication is key for every success.

TRUST

Doing good or doing bad ? We are humans, and we make mistakes. The most important thing is to learn from it.

COMMUNITY

Players / devs ? We are all part of the same community. Be respectful, sincere, empathic, and thrive together.
